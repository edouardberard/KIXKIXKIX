KIXKIXKIX — VAR

About

This tool was made by Edouard Berard as part of a project conducted at the Glasgow School of Art, BA Interaction Design.
It uses P5.js, ML5.js and the PoseNet machine learning model. This project explores the limit of computer vision
to generate a font from your posture. The stiffer you are the sharper the font will be.

The variable font is open-source and can be downloaded here.
